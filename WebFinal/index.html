Blazers Finish Road-Trip 4-1

After the 103-91 victory over the New York Knicks, the Trail Blazers will return home with a 13-8 record. Lillard finished the win over the Knicks with 32 points on 50% shooting. The team will be back in the Moda Center Thursday November 30 to take on the Milwaukee Bucks.
